RestockDex Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 8, 2026

RestockDex is a Pokemon stock, queue alert, release calendar, and news dashboard. It does not have user accounts and it does not sell personal information.

What RestockDex Uses

  • The app requests notification permission only if you turn on alerts.
  • Your notification setting and recent alert timing may be stored on your device so alerts do not repeat too often.
  • RestockDex connects to the RestockDex API to load shop monitors, product drops, release calendar items, and news links.

Hosting and Technical Data

Railway, Vercel, app stores, browsers, and linked retailer or news websites may process standard technical data such as IP address, device type, browser details, request times, and error logs.

Third-Party Links

Retailer and news links open third-party websites. Those websites have their own privacy policies and are not controlled by RestockDex.

Notifications

You can turn notifications off in your browser or phone settings at any time.

Contact and Affiliation

For support, use the contact details provided with the app listing. RestockDex is not affiliated with Pokemon, Nintendo, The Pokemon Company, or the retailers shown in the app.
